## What is metrota?
Simply put, just word-play', I was messin around with some branding around the word **metro**, then soon realized how many companies and/or brands in the US that use ~~metro~~. Then soon I discoverd this lovely Zola theme [tabi](https://www.getzola.org/themes/tabi/). Viola, "metro-tabi" morphed into metrota.
